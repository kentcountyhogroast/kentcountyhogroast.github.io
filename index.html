Kent County Hog Roast is a family run business based in Borough Green, Kent. We specialise in providing everything you need; from the roasting machine and the cooking gas, to the succulent hog itself for high quality hog roast and BBQ catering in Kent and the surrounding areas. Our aim is to provide the highest quality experience, whether it is a wedding, private party or corporate event. We have a great team on hand to prepare, cook and serve the hog roast to your guests, meaning you will not need to worry about a thing on the day! We are flexible in our approach. If you have an enquiry outside of what is listed on the website, please get in touch and we can work together on a bespoke package. If you would prefer an alternative to a hog, we have a number of different meats including beef, lamb, wild boar and venison available which also make a perfect roast.
